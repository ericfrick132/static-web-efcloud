Project 6: Molo - Marina Management Transformation

Client: Molo
Industry: Marina Management
Project Overview:
Our team spearheaded the technological overhaul of Molo, a comprehensive marina management system. The project involved migrating their legacy system from an outdated .NET framework to a modern, cloud-native .NET Core architecture. This transition not only streamlined operations but also leveraged the scalability and reliability of cloud services.

Key Contributions:

Cloud Migration: Transitioned the entire infrastructure to a cloud-native environment, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing overhead costs.
Development Process Optimization: Implemented Agile methodologies, defining the SDLC and organizing work into structured sprints. This shift drastically improved project transparency and team productivity.
Marina Management Solutions: Our developments supported the diverse needs of marinas, boatyards, and yacht clubs, enabling them to manage slips, dry racks, and storage buildings effectively.
Service and Repair Management: Enhanced the service management tools to facilitate small boat repairs and megayacht refits, incorporating features for managing work orders, job estimates, and parts inventory.
Outcomes:
The modernization of Molo has not only revitalized their operational workflow but also empowered them to handle marina management with unprecedented efficiency. Our work has positioned Molo as a leader in the industry, significantly enhancing their service delivery and customer satisfaction.
